About us

Welcome to Brew & Bloom Cafe – where passion for coffee meets the art of community and comfort! Founded in 2017 in the heart of Lovely Professional University, Our Cafe was born out of a love for creating exceptional coffee experiences. Our goal is simple: to serve quality coffee and delicious food in a welcoming space that feels like home.

Our Mission

At Brew & Bloom Cafe, our mission is to bring people together through great coffee, fresh food, and meaningful moments. Whether you're here for a quick cup to-go, a meeting spot with friends, or a cozy nook to work, we want to make every visit special.

Visit Us

Drop by for a taste of what we’re brewing! We’re open Monday to Saturday, located at LPU Campus. We look forward to welcoming you soon!
